ReFaceStudio Privacy Policy

ReFaceStudio ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or physical address.

However, the App processes images uploaded by users, which may contain faces, solely for the purpose of providing AI-based image transformation features.

2. Face Data Handling

Uploaded images containing faces are used only to provide requested image processing features, including but not limited to:

  • Image enhancement
  • Aging simulation
  • Gender transformation
  • Makeup simulation
  • Other AI-based image editing features

The App does NOT:

  • Perform facial recognition for identification purposes
  • Create or store biometric identifiers
  • Store facial feature templates
  • Use images for identity verification
  • Use uploaded images for AI model training

3. Data Processing and Storage

Uploaded images are temporarily processed using secure RunPod Serverless infrastructure.

Processed images are securely stored in Cloudflare R2 storage.

The App does not sell, share, or disclose user images to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Retention

All uploaded and processed images are automatically deleted within 24 hours after processing.

No long-term storage of face data occurs.

5. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. Advertising identifiers and device information may be collected for ad delivery and optimization.
